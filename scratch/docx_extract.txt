Entry requires a Foundation certificate or equivalent work experience. Courses run 12–20 weeks, include capstone projects, and align with professional certifications (AWS Solutions Architect, CISSP, Azure Expert, etc.). Learners should have 2+ years of industry experience or hold an Associate-level certificate.Enterprise — SETA NQF Level 7–8Executive and senior leadership programmes. Targets CTOs, CISOs, CDOs, senior engineers, and strategic leaders responsible for digital transformation at organisational scale. Delivered in cohort or modular format over 16–24 weeks. Includes executive workshops, industry mentors, and board-presentation practicals.Regulatory & Accreditation FrameworkSouth African Qualifications Authority (SAQA)All GDA qualifications will be registered on the National Qualifications Framework (NQF) via SAQA. Each course maps to formal NQF unit standards with clearly stated exit-level outcomes, assessment criteria, and credit values. This enables learners to accumulate credits toward formal qualifications recognised by employers and government.Quality Council for Trades and Occupations (QCTO)Foundation, Associate, and Professional courses will be submitted to QCTO for occupational qualification registration. This places GDA graduates on recognised occupational pathways relevant to ICT, digital media, and business services — sectors identified by the South African Government's Operation Vulindlela and Digital Economy Strategy.SETA AlignmentCourses are mapped to unit standards under MICT SETA (Media, Information and Communication Technology) and SERVICES SETA where applicable. This enables employer Skills Development Levies (SDL) to fund learner bursaries, and qualifies GDA as a provider for W&RSETA, BANKSETA, and other sector SETAs.Pan-African RecognitionCurriculum content and assessments are designed to be relevant across Sub-Saharan African markets. The academy will pursue mutual recognition agreements with equivalent bodies in SADC, ECOWAS, and EAC member states, supporting learner mobility and cross-border employment opportunities in the growing African digital economy.SADC Qualifications Framework (SADCQF) alignment pursued from Year 2ECOWAS digital skills framework compatibility built into all Foundation and Associate curriculaPartnership pathway with African Union Commission's Digital Transformation StrategyContent localised for South Africa with adaptation guides for Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, and ZimbabweCourse Descriptions — All 28 CoursesEach course below is mapped to a SETA NQF level and designed to stack progressively within its track.
